Kodomo for Charity

Kodomo for Mongolian Children

The meaning of Le Kodomo: Kodomo is the Japanese word for child, meaning inspiration, future and innocence. The Kodomo project is about more than the art, it is also about supporting small not-for-profit organizations that help and support less fortunate children in the world. These organisations are not very well known but were discovered by Martina during her travels and she has made a commitment to contribute to their lives in what ways she can. Kodomo for Charity: For the children of Mongolia The first Kodomo for Charity project will be to support the children of Morono in Mongolia. All the proceeds of the project will be used to purchase educational material for the children, and to provide them with English courses and free access to education. The ultimate goal is to see their dreams fulfilled, in a future far from poverty.
